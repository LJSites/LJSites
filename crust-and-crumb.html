about

We started in a converted garage in 2021 with a single deck oven and one rule: only bake what we'd be proud to take home.

That rule still applies. We don't have a wholesale list, we don't ship, and we don't make wedding cakes. We bake bread and a small board of pastries each morning, and when it's gone we close the shop and start the next day's mix.

Our flour comes from Grist & Toll up the hill in Pasadena. Our butter is from Straus. Our espresso is Heart out of Portland. Everything else, we figure out.
