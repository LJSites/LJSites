— how we got here

A converted garage
and one rule that still holds.

We started in a converted garage in 2021 with a single deck oven and one rule: only bake what we'd be proud to take home.

That rule still applies. We don't have a wholesale list, we don't ship, we don't make wedding cakes. We bake bread and a small board of pastries each morning, and when it's gone we close the shop and start tomorrow's mix.

Flour from Grist & Toll up the hill in Pasadena. Butter from Straus. Espresso from Heart out of Portland. Everything else we figure out.
