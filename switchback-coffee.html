§ 03 · The roaster

Bought direct, roasted Tuesdays,
in your bag by Friday.

We started in 2018 with a 1-kg sample roaster in a Glendale industrial unit. Eight months later we bought the Loring, signed the lease on the Colorado storefront, and never looked back.

Every lot on the bar comes from a single farm, traceably. We buy what we taste, in quantities we can finish before it stales, and we tell you exactly when it was roasted on the bag.

If you want to know more about a coffee than the tasting notes on the menu — ask. We'll tell you.
