// about

A two-person studio working with seasonal California growers — and one small flower farm we love in Carpinteria.

We started in 2020, working out of a converted bungalow on Lemoyne Street. The studio you see today is the third move and the last one — we own the storefront now, and we're staying.

We work mostly by appointment, but if you see the front door open on a Friday afternoon, it means we have bouquets ready to go. Come in.
