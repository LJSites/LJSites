About the shop

My grandfather opened this shop with a single Bridgeport in 1998. Today we run nine machines under one roof — and we still answer the phone ourselves.

We take work other shops turn away: weird materials, low quantities, drawings that aren't quite finished. If you send us a sketch and a deadline, you'll get a quote back the same day, and a part that gauges on the first try.

DFARS-compliant material sourcing. AS9100 in progress.
