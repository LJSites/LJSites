03 · ABOUT THE SHOP

Third generation. Same phone number.

My grandfather opened this shop with a single Bridgeport in 1998. Today we run nine machines under one roof — and we still answer the phone ourselves.

We take work other shops turn away: weird materials, low quantities, drawings that aren't quite finished. Send us a sketch and a deadline, you'll get a quote the same day and a part that gauges on the first try.

DFARS-compliant material sourcing. AS9100 in progress. Same-day rush for parts under 4 inches.
