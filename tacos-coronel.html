about

Pop bought this corner stand for $4,200 in 2011. We've added one chair and zero shortcuts since.

Our masa is from Tamoa in Mexico City. We grind salsas in a molcajete, not a blender. The trompo runs from 11 to whenever the pastor is gone — usually 7pm on weekdays, 9 on Saturdays.

No app, no delivery, no DoorDash. You come, you order at the window, you eat at the standing counter or take it home. That's it.
