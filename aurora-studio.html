§ 01 — ABOUT

Aurora opened in 2019 above the bookshop on Sunset Boulevard. Two chairs, by appointment, and a single guarantee: you will never sit in a chair you didn't pick.

Mira — the founder — trained in Brooklyn, apprenticed in Tokyo, and came home to Silver Lake to do the kind of colour that grows out gracefully instead of giving you a deadline. She does almost all the colour herself. She'll talk you out of a single-process if it isn't right for your hair, and into a glaze you didn't know you needed.

Devon joined in 2022, sharp barbering background, an eye for shape that flatters how you actually wear your hair on a Wednesday. Between the two of them, the chairs are full a month out — and they like it that way.

We aren't a franchise. We aren't a chain. We are a small room with very good light, a real coffee setup, the records you'd hope for, and an answer to the colour question you've been carrying around for years.
