// about

Aurora opened in 2019 above the bookshop on Sunset. Two chairs, by appointment, and a guarantee: you'll never sit in a chair you didn't pick.

Mira (the founder) trained in Brooklyn, apprenticed in Tokyo, and came home to Silver Lake to do the kind of color that grows out gracefully instead of giving you a deadline.

Devon joined in 2022 — sharp barbering background, an eye for shape that flatters how you actually wear your hair on a Wednesday.
