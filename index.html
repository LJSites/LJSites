03 · How it works

A pipeline, not a sales funnel.

Every LJ Sites mockup ships with hand-tuned typography, real photos pulled live from Google Places, an embedded map, and a working call button. We build the mockup speculatively — you only sign on once you've seen the work.

01 · Discover

Find the business

We sweep California for small businesses without a website of their own — places whose only digital front door is a Google Maps pin. Real places that deserve a real one.

02 · Design

Build the mockup

Photos, hours, menu, testimonies — all real, all hand-set. One archetype per site, picked to fit the place. No templates.

03 · Ship

Publish to the web

Within minutes the mockup is live on a clean ljsites.github.io URL. We send the owner the link — anywhere in California — so they can see it, click it, call themselves from it.

04 · Care

We stay around

Once you sign on, we wire up your domain, set up your email, and look after the site month to month — content edits, refreshes, the small fixes most studios charge by the hour for. See Pricing for what's included.

04 · About

A two-person studio
in LA.

Jimmy & Levin. We kept walking into restaurants and shops we loved that didn't show up on Google. We got tired of that.

So we built a pipeline: find local places without a real online front door, ship them a single-page mockup as a sales pitch, and let the work speak for itself. No cold outreach, no pitch decks, no contracts to sign before you've seen what we'd actually make for you.

Every site is hand-tuned. We don't ship templates. We don't ghost you after launch — we keep your site running month to month and pick up the phone when something needs to change. We started in LA and now serve businesses anywhere in California.
