§ 01 · How it works

A pipeline, not a sales funnel.

Every LJ Sites mockup is a single self-contained HTML file: hand-tuned typography, real photos when we have them, an embedded map, and a call button. We build it speculatively. You only pay if you decide to keep it.

01 · Discover

Find the business.

We sweep the LA area for small businesses with no website — or only a Facebook page. Real places that deserve a real front door.

02 · Design

Build the mockup.

Photos, hours, menu, testimonies — all real, all hand-set. One archetype per site, picked to fit the place. No templates.

03 · Ship

Publish to the web.

Within minutes the mockup is live on a clean URL. We hand it to the owner. They can see it, click it, call themselves from it.

04 · Hand over

It's theirs.

If they want it: flat fee, no monthly. We transfer the repo, point the domain, and stay on call for the first month. Otherwise it stays online as our portfolio.
