About the shop

Same family. Same phone.
Same answer.

My dad opened this shop in 2003 with two bays and a coffee maker. The coffee maker is still here.

We don't have a fancy waiting room. We have honest techs, a clean shop floor, and the kind of regulars who bring their kids' cars in when those kids are old enough to drive.

If the repair isn't worth doing — because the car isn't worth it — we'll tell you that, too. We'd rather lose the ticket than your trust.
